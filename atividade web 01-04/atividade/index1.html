    Cotia, Brasil

    Cotia is a municipality in the state of São Paulo, located in the Metropolitan Region of São Paulo. The city lies along the banks of the Cotia River, a tributary of the Tietê River. Cotia is an area of expansion within the Metropolitan Region of São Paulo. The economy of Cotia is diverse, with industrial and agricultural sectors standing out. The city boasts many tourist attractions, marked by faith, adventure, ecology, culture, and leisure. Cotia preserves waterfalls, the Atlantic Forest, and wide spaces for religious gatherings.
